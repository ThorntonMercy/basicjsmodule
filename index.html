About VirtualPups 2.0

Long ago in the 2000's, a great website called VirtualPups ruled the online dog game world. A dedicated few stuck around for over a decade, all the way to the end. Now, VirtualPups 2.0 is here. We offer a similar playstyle to the original VirtualPups. Here, we breed, train, and compete a wide variety of dog breeds. We offer all sorts of customizations, creating nearly limitless options for players to have a truly one-of-a-kind experience.

We've opened registration to the general public! Please note that we're still in the beta testing stage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our website is completely free to play with optional upgrades. These upgrades may include such things as account perks, specialized dog colors, access to premium forums, and more. Upgrades are not essential to the enjoyment of the website or to play and participate in the general game play.

We have over 100 breeds currently available. We do not have or support the breeding of "mutts" or mixed breeds, feral breeds, or other mythical creatures.

Our community is very inclusive. We foster a safe environment with great moderators who track and remove anyone engaging in harmful ideologies like racism, sexism, and other intolerant bigotry. We have automatic scanning in private and public messaging for predatory behavior.

Our staff regularly host free, site-wide games and special events. These events sometimes release limited-time rewards, such as specially colored dogs or upgrades.
